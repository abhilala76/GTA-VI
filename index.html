GTA VI - Welcome to Vice City Again

After years of speculation, Rockstar finally confirmed GTA VI. The neon-soaked streets of Vice City await, promising bigger heists, dynamic characters, and the chaos that defines the Grand Theft Auto universe.

What's New?

GTA VI introduces new mechanics like dynamic weather systems, more interactive NPCs, and dual protagonists. Rumors hint at a map stretching beyond Vice City into neighboring regions, pushing the boundaries of open-world gameplay.

Story & Characters

Speculation suggests the narrative will follow two main characters navigating the criminal underworld, blending classic GTA humor with modern storytelling. Rockstar is reportedly focusing on more diverse perspectives and character arcs.
